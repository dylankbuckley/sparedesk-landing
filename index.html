About SpareDesk


SpareDesk was created to solve a simple problem: Finding a great place to work, and get working their as quickly as possible. We aren't the first to try make this task for entrepreneurs and freelancers easier, but we believe our partnerships with coworking spaces globally, and personal knowledge of the coworking and freelancers world, puts us in a great position to execute it perfectly.
